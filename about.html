Our mission

IPI works to make parenting preparation clear, fair, and practical. The institute develops curriculum standards, assessment pathways, and support resources for families across different cultural and legal contexts.

Care

Every programme starts from child wellbeing, family dignity, and practical day-to-day support.

Evidence

Courses are reviewed against developmental science, public health guidance, and field practice.

Access

Services are designed for multilingual, disability-aware, and regionally adaptable delivery.

What IPI provides

1
